Abby's Kitchen

Rustic Chicken

A zesty broiled chicken seasoned with organic spices picked from our own backyard. Topped with garlic potatoes and asparagus. Drizzled with a spicy honey glaze.

Jasmine Salmon

A unique combination of jasmine flavor with habanero chillies makes this dish our client's favorite. The salmon is grilled on a low fire and served with a side of jasmine rice.

Shrimp Al Vapor

Hand picked by our local fishermen, this dish is flavorful and fresh. The "al vapor" technique allows the shrimps to store their juice while being cooked at a low heat. This flavorful dish comes with are unique fettuccini carbonara.
